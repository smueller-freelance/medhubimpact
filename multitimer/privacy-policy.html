Privacy Policy - Multi Timer

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Multi Timer ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Device Identifier: We generate a random, anonymous device ID to identify your device. This ID is not linked to your personal identity.

App Usage: We record when you open the app to understand usage patterns and improve our service.

Timer Data: Your timers are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect and use data for personalized advertising. You can learn more about Google's practices at Google Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

Your timer configurations are stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms.

Anonymous usage data is stored on our servers to help improve the app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with:

  • Google AdMob for advertising purposes
  • Our cloud infrastructure providers for app functionality

Your Rights

You can delete all local app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
