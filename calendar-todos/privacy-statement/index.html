Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

Overview

Calendar Todos ("the App") is a simple calendar and task management application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

All your todo items, preferences, and settings are stored locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Data Storage

Your data is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. If you uninstall the App, all data will be permanently deleted.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13 (COPPA) or under 16 (GDPR).

Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Since all data remains on your device and we have no access to it, you maintain full control. You can delete all data by uninstalling the App.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
