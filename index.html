From the Frontlines to the Future of Healthcare

Our story starts with a nurse who deeply cared about her patients—but she kept running into the same roadblocks: fragmented care, poor communication, and outdated systems. Hoping to make a broader impact, she moved from clinical work to healthcare education, then into health policy. No matter where she went, the problems followed.

That’s when she teamed up with her partner, a software engineer. Together, they began building smart, user-friendly healthcare digital solutions designed to solve real problems and improve how care is delivered, accessed, and experienced.

Our mission – empower healthcare professionals and patients with technology that actually works in the real world.
