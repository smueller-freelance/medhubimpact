How to Delete Your Account

If you wish to permanently delete your Be Autogenic account and all associated data, you have two options:

Option 1: Delete from Within the App (Recommended)

Quick and Easy: You can delete your account directly from the Be Autogenic mobile app.

  1. Open the Be Autogenic app
  2. Navigate to your Profile screen
  3. Tap on "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision in the dialog that appears
  5. Your account will be deleted immediately

Option 2: Email Us

If you prefer, you can request account deletion by contacting us directly:

  • Email: support@medhubimpact.com
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Include: The email address associated with your Be Autogenic account

We will process your request and confirm the deletion within 7 business days.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your user profile and account information
  • All personalized autogenic training sessions you created
  • Your session history and playback preferences
  • Your subscription and payment information (if applicable)
  • Any stored preferences or settings
  • Your authentication credentials

Grace Period & Data Retention

30-Day Grace Period: When you request account deletion, your account becomes immediately inaccessible (you will be logged out and cannot log back in). However, your data is retained for 30 days in case you change your mind.

During this grace period, you can contact us at support@medhubimpact.com to cancel the deletion request and restore your account.

After the grace period: Your data will be permanently deleted, including:

  • All personal information and account data from our databases
  • Your Auth0 authentication account
  • All audio files from our storage servers

Legal retention: We may retain minimal transaction records (without personal identifiers) only as required by Romanian law for tax and accounting purposes. Such records contain no personal data and cannot be used to identify you.

⚠️ Important: After the 30-day grace period expires, account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered. Please make sure you have saved any content you wish to keep before requesting deletion.

What Happens Immediately

When you request account deletion:

  • You will be immediately logged out of the app
  • You will no longer be able to log in or access your account
  • Your account becomes inaccessible to all app functions
  • Any active subscriptions will be cancelled
  • A 30-day grace period begins

After the Grace Period

After 30 days, all your data will be permanently deleted. You can create a new account in the future if you wish to use Be Autogenic again.
